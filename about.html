Our Story

Building Trust Since 2010

QN Pharma began with a simple yet powerful vision — to make quality medicines accessible and affordable for everyone in Bihar. Founded by Altaf Emteyaz in the heart of Nai Sadak, Patna City, QN Pharma has grown into one of the most reliable wholesale pharmaceutical distributors in the region.

Over the years, we have built strong partnerships with leading pharmaceutical manufacturers and brands, enabling us to offer a comprehensive range of surgical supplies, ethical medicines, and generic drugs — all under one roof, at the best market prices.

Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of hundreds of hospitals, clinics, and chemists across Patna and surrounding districts.

Meet The Proprietor

The Face Behind QN Pharma

Your Reliable Pharma Partner
Altaf Emteyaz

Altaf Emteyaz is the founder and proprietor of QN Pharma, based in Nai Sadak, Patna City, Bihar. With over 15 years of hands-on experience in pharmaceutical wholesale distribution, he has established QN Pharma as a name synonymous with quality, reliability, and trust.

His deep knowledge of the pharmaceutical industry, strong relationships with manufacturers, and dedication to customer service have been the cornerstone of QN Pharma's growth and reputation throughout Bihar.
